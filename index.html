Our Story

Born From a Love for Street Food

What started as a simple passion for perfecting the humble samosa has grown into something extraordinary. At House of Samosas, we believe that street food isn't just about taste — it's about experience, innovation, and the joy of discovering something unexpected.

Every samosa in our kitchen is a flavor experiment — from the boldness of Pizza Samosa to the elegance of Malai Paneer. We don't follow recipes; we create experiences. Each bite is crafted to surprise, delight, and leave you craving more.
